Welcome to my photography website, I hope you like my photos.

My background is in Software Development and digital imagery. I studied Information Technology at college and university and I worked as a software developer in Thermal Imaging for almost 10 years.

I've owned SLR cameras for the last 20 years, starting with a Canon 35mm film camera. I have been upgrading lenses and camera bodies over the years and I am very happy with my current photography gear.

For many years my main interest in photography was motorsport and automotive. More recently I am passionate about all types of photography, including wildlife, nature, macro, astrophotography, fashion, product and property.

Everyday I'm either reading or watching YouTube about photography. I am always wanting to learn more, improve my photography and try new things. I am also interested in videography and edited videos for YouTube.

I have been making websites for many years and wrote this website as a simple portfolio website, showcasing a small number of my photos.

For my motorsport photos I developed MotorsportPics.com which rates very well on Google. It is currently hosted on PhotoShelter but I am looking to redevelop it on SquareSpace.
